About Burj Tours & Travels

The leader among travel agencies

Burj Tours & Travel Company Ltd is a full-service travel agency serving business and leisure clients with professional, friendly, and efficient organization of their outbound and inbound travel needs.

We are accredited by the Kenya Association of Travel Agents (KATA), Kenya Association of Tour Operators (KATO), and the International Air Transport Association (IATA).

Our head office is in Nairobi, Kenya. We are powered by an experienced, multilingual, and highly qualified team, led by our Executive Director Mr. Edward Martin Nabibya. Together, we combine industry expertise with youthful enthusiasm, creativity, and a deep knowledge of the travel and tourism industry.

To provide the best possible quality services, Burj maintains strong relationships with international organizations and suppliers worldwide to ensure we meet and exceed client expectations.

Our safari drivers and guides are experts on East Africa’s birds, wildlife, people, and culture, making every journey memorable and informative.

At Burj, our corporate values are built on sustainability, innovation, superior quality, and friendly service — ensuring every traveler’s journey is rewarding.
